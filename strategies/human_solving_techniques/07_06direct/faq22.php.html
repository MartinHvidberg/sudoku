When pasting from the clipboard, what text formats does SadMan Sudoku accept?

SadMan Sudoku can accept a wide variety of formats when pasting text from the clipboard (and don't forget you can also paste BMP, JPG, PNG or GIF images). This means you can copy a puzzle from many online sources, and paste it directly into Sudoku - in most cases, there is no need to manually adjust it beforehand.

These examples all correspond to the puzzle below, although those formats without pencil marks will only have the puzzle clues of course.
