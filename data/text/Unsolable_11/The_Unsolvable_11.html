The "Unsolvable" 11

The program is also easily capable of solving all of the so-called "Unsolvable 11" from the SudokuList website using the simple logic heuristics described earlier, see http://www.sudoku.org.uk/bifurcation.htm.

Only puzzles #1 and #7 require Heuristic 1+ to open up to allow chaining up to lengths 4 and 5, respectively.
